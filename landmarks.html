Schönbrunn Palace

Vienna's historic palace, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most important cultural monuments in Austria.

Hofburg Palace

The former imperial palace in Vienna, now the official residence and workplace of the President of Austria.

Melk Abbey

A Benedictine abbey above the town of Melk on a rocky outcrop overlooking the Danube river, known for its stunning baroque architecture.

Hohensalzburg Fortress

One of the largest medieval castles in Europe, perched atop the Festungsberg in Salzburg, offering panoramic views of the city.
