Culture of Austria

Explore the rich cultural heritage of Austria, including its music, art, and traditions.

Music

Austria is renowned for its classical music heritage, home to composers like Mozart, Beethoven, and Strauss. The Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra is one of the most prestigious orchestras in the world.

Art

Austria has a rich tradition in visual arts, with famous painters like Gustav Klimt and Egon Schiele. The Belvedere Palace in Vienna showcases many masterpieces from these artists.

Traditions

From traditional costumes to folk dances, Austria's cultural traditions are vibrant and celebrated. The Vienna Opera Ball is a famous event showcasing Austrian culture and elegance.
