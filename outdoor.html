Outdoor Activities in Austria

Experience thrilling outdoor adventures in Austria’s stunning landscapes, including skiing, hiking, and more!

Popular Activities

Skiing: Austria is home to some of the best ski resorts in the world, including St. Anton and Kitzbühel.
Hiking: Explore the breathtaking trails in the Austrian Alps, suitable for all levels of hikers.
Mountain Biking: Discover scenic biking routes with stunning views of the mountains.
Paragliding: Experience the thrill of soaring above the stunning landscapes of Austria.
